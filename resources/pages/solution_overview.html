The algorithms we use here to recommend movies rely on movies other users have seen and rated, as well as similar movies that a user has seen. The following is an overview of how they work.

Content based
Here we made recommendations based on how similar the properties or features of a movie are to other movies. We used movies with similar themes or have similar actors and or/directors to predict what the user will like.

Collaborative based
This algorithm is based on past interactions between users and the movies in the database. We make recommendations based on movies that those users gave a high rating.
